Leader

Pingping Liu

liupp@jlu.edu.cn

Pingping Liu is a Professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Jilin University, and a Ph.D. supervisor. She is a member of the China Computer Federation (CCF). Her research interests include low-level vision and image enhancement, metric learning, medical image analysis, infrared small target detection, and embedded AI. Over the past three years, she has published 21 papers in top-tier international conferences and journals (CCF-A/B/C; CAS Q1/Q2), including 11 as first author or corresponding author. She serves on the editorial board of the Journal of Jilin University (Engineering and Technology Edition), and has long been a reviewer for top CCF conferences such as IJCAI, AAAI, NeurIPS, and ACM MM, as well as flagship IEEE journals including TIP, TCYB, and TNNLS, and flagship ACM journals such as TMM and TKDD. She received the Second Prize of the China General Chamber of Commerce Science and Technology Progress Award (2024, ranked 1st) and the Second Prize of the China Invention Association Invention & Innovation Entrepreneurship Award (2024, ranked 3rd). She holds 31 authorized Chinese invention patents and has led multiple research projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation, the Jilin Provincial Natural Science Foundation, the Jilin Provincial Young Scientists Fund, and key R&D programs in the industrial sector of Jilin Province.
